About Me

With over 16 years of experience in construction planning, project management, and logistics, Ali Sufyan is a Senior Planner at McLaren Construction Group. Based in London, Ali has an extensive background in managing large-scale projects, including commercial buildings, data centers, and mixed-use developments. Ali holds an MBA in Innovation Management from Coventry University, a Bachelor's in Information Technology, and an NVQ Level 6 in Construction Management. He is also certified in Powerproject BIM and various safety training schemes. Ali’s expertise encompasses planning, scheduling, and ensuring quality and safety standards across numerous high-profile projects.
