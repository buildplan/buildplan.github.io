About Me

With over 15 years in the construction industry, I'm a highly experienced Senior Planner at McLaren Construction Group, based in London. I've been fortunate to work on a diverse range of projects, from local authority residential schemes to high-rise boutique hotels, and from data centers and shopping centres to large-scale exhibition facilities and city-centre office buildings. My expertise lies in managing these large-scale projects, ensuring efficient delivery while adhering to the highest quality and standards.

My educational background fuels this expertise. I hold an MBA in Innovation Management, which fuels my strategic thinking, and a Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology. Additionally, I hold an NVQ Level 6 in Construction Management, providing a robust technical foundation. This unique blend of education and experience enables me to approach project planning with fresh perspectives, finding innovative solutions.
Beyond technical proficiency, I am committed to excellence. As a member of the Chartered Institute of Building (CIOB), I continuously strive to stay current with industry best practices and contribute to the advancement of our profession.
